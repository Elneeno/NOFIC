WELCOME

The National Obstetrics Fistula Centre, Ningi is a North East regional centre established in the year 2013 when the Federal Government of Nigeria took over the services of the former Visico Vaginal Fistula (VVF) centre, Ningi General Hospital, Bauchi State. The core mandate of the centre is the provision of free services for the prevention, treatment, rehabilitation training and research on VVF in the North East Zone. This is a strategic move by Government considering the fact that the North east has the worst incidence of VVF in the country especially with the current insurgency. The Government in its wisdom decided to establish this centre to locate and treat the patients free and if possible, prevent when it is not too late. The task is enormous.


Patients suffering from VVF are mostly faced with challenges especially when we reckon that poverty and ignorance contribute a lot to the incidence of VVF prevalence. They are discriminate against and at times ejected from their homes. The stigma is so great it derives some of them to the extremes like prostitution and depression. They are a very serious vulnerable group.

Patients suffering from Fistula undergoes and endures so much discriminations and pains because in the first instance, majority of them are from poor and remote backgrounds and so factors like poverty, lack of literacy combined with the disease make them very disadvantaged and rejected that they normally require interventions to survive even after surgery.
